Format

Teams of four compete in buzzer rounds and team problem-solving.

Rules

Follow the official US Department of Energy Science Bowl rules and local modifications.

Resources

Past questions, practice packets, and scoring sheets.

Volunteer

We rely on volunteers for moderators, scorers, and proctors. Volunteers receive training and refreshments.

Volunteer Roles

Moderator, Scorekeeper, Runner, Check-in Desk. Shifts last ~4-6 hours.

About

UCSD Science Bowl is a student-run event hosted by UC San Diego to inspire and challenge high school students in STEM.

Our Mission

Encourage interest in STEM through friendly competition and community.

History

Founded by students and faculty supporters — decades of regional competition.
